Reformats messy Kiro-CLI output into clean prose, lists, and tables. Detects ordered and unordered lists, strips blockquote prefixes, softens em-dashes into commas or hyphens, unwraps soft line breaks, and supports inline markdown (bold, italic, code, links) when rendered as HTML. Output can be copied as plain text or as minimal HTML suitable for pasting into Outlook or Slack.
